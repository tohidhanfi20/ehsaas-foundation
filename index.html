With a special focus on supporting marginalized and minority communities, especially the Muslim community, we aim to bring lasting change through collective effort and service.

About us

Who We Are

Ehsaas Foundation is a socially committed NGO formed by responsible citizens who believe in community development through compassion and service. We support underprivileged individuals and families by providing assistance in health, education, charity, cultural development, and social welfare.

Our Vision

To build an empowered, educated, healthy, and culturally enriched community where every individual receives equal opportunities to grow, learn and thrive.

Our Mission

  • Support needy families through charity and welfare programs
  • Promote education and skill development among children and youth
  • Conduct health awareness drives and medical camps
  • Preserve cultural values and promote social harmony
  • Empower women, youth, orphans, and widows
  • Extend help during emergencies, natural calamities and crises

Our Work

We design programs around the most pressing needs of the community—ensuring dignity, opportunity, and wellbeing for every individual.

Social Welfare & Charity

  • Distribution of ration kits and food support
  • Financial assistance to widows, orphans, and poor families
  • Emergency relief support

Education & Skill Development

  • Educational guidance and scholarships (based on need)
  • Career counselling sessions
  • Support for school and college students

Health & Wellness Initiatives

  • Free health check-up camps
  • Medical awareness programs
  • Helping families in medical emergencies

Cultural & Community Development

  • Organizing cultural events and traditional programs
  • Promoting unity, peace, and brotherhood
  • Celebrating important social and cultural days

Women & Youth Empowerment

  • Training workshops
  • Skill development programs
  • Awareness sessions for self-reliance
